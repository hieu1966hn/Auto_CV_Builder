# P08 - Definition of Done (DoD) cho AutoCV MVP

## DoD
1. Nguoi dung nhap duoc thong tin co ban va thay preview cap nhat.
2. Cac truong du lieu chinh hien thi dung vi tri tren CV.
3. Khong co loi nghiem trong khi nhap/sua/xoa noi dung.
4. Giao dien su dung duoc tren desktop va mobile co ban.
5. Co checklist test thu cong va da test pass cac muc quan trong.
6. Co tai lieu ghi ro hanh vi hien tai va gioi han cua ban MVP.

## Dieu kien pass
- Tat ca test muc uu tien cao pass.
- Khong co bug blocker/open critical.
