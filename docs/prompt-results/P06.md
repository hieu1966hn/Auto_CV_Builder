# P06 - Checklist test thu cong cho MVP AutoCV

## Muc tieu
Tao checklist de nguoi khong biet code van test duoc ban MVP.

## Checklist
1. Mo trang web, kiem tra form hien day du cac truong co ban.
2. Nhap ho ten, email, phone -> xem preview co cap nhat dung khong.
3. Nhap ky nang tach boi dau phay -> preview hien dung tung muc.
4. Nhap kinh nghiem nhieu dong -> preview hien dung tung dong.
5. Bo trong email hoac ho ten -> he thong can thong bao ro (neu da co validate).
6. Sua thong tin da nhap -> preview phai cap nhat ngay, khong bi tre.
7. Thu tren man hinh hep (mobile) -> giao dien van doc duoc.
8. Refresh trang -> xac nhan hanh vi du lieu (giu hay mat) dung voi mong doi.

## Ket qua mong doi
- Khong co crash.
- Preview dong bo voi input.
- Cac truong quan trong khong bi bo qua (neu co validate).
