# P04 - Danh sach MVP theo thu tu uu tien

## MVP bat buoc
1. Form nhap thong tin co ban (ho ten, email, phone, vi tri).
2. O ky nang/kinh nghiem/hoc van.
3. Live preview cap nhat theo input.
4. Validate co ban (khong de trong truong quan trong).
5. UI de doc tren desktop/mobile.

## MVP nen co ngay sau do
6. Nut reset form.
7. Muc thong bao loi ro rang, de hieu.
8. Mot mau CV don gian, de on dinh.

## MVP phat trien tiep
9. Xuat PDF A4.
10. Chon mau CV nang cao.
11. Luu/tai lai du lieu.
